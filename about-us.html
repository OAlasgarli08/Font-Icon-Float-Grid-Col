Azercell Life

Our purpose

Easing connectivity, empowering lives!



Azercell is the only company in Azerbaijan and CIS region which has been awarded Platinum Certificate of International “Investors in People” Standard. The mobile operator is the only company in the country to receive Gold Award in nominations of "Company of the Year" and “The Most Innovative Company of the Year” from the International Business Award STEVIE.



Rapidly expanding 4G network of Azercell covers nearly 60 regions of the country, including Baku and Absheron peninsula.

Four pillars of our strategy:

Continue to be the leading telco in Azerbaijan

Keeping the customer at the heart of our operations we gain trust and ensure sustainable improvement. We aim to offer even more advanced customer services and an extensive range of innovative products.

Accelerate further development of industry while generating value

Providing our customers with simple solutions and smooth connectivity. We aspire to enable subscribers to embrace technological future that offers endless opportunities.

Foster innovation to empower lives

Becoming a preferred provider for full range of smart services by pushing further digital inclusion.

Be one of Azerbaijan's most desired places to work

Creating an environment that allows to deliver operational efficiency and innovativeness. We attract and develop talented and committed human capital who strives for excellence and continuous improvement.

Innovations

Pioneering mobile operator to introduce a number of innovations


Azercell has always been at the forefront of telecoms innovation in Azerbaijan. We were the first to introduce mobile internet services, GSM technology, 4G connectivity, the first 5G network in test mode, Azercell Fintech, which combines banking operations with mobile communication capabilities, M2M services, advanced payment systems, a 24/7 call center hotline, 7/7 face-to-face customer services and all-in-one Express offices, mobile and online customer care services and customer services through social media, “ASAN Imza” mobile e-signature service and other solutions.
